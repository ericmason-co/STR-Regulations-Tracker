    About LawfulStay

    LawfulStay was founded by a coalition of real estate investors, short-term rental hosts, and data analysts who experienced firsthand the difficulty of navigating fragmented municipal codes. We built this platform to bring clarity, transparency, and structure to the complex web of local laws.

    Our research team tracks council meetings, draft ordinances, and zoning adjustments daily. We verify every registry source, platform mandate, and penalty schedule so that stakeholders can identify risk, evaluate markets, and run compliance checks with confidence.

    Our Data Promise: 100% Sourced, verified, and community-enriched. When local laws shift, we update our records to ensure you always have the most current compliance outlook.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How often is the regulations database updated?

    We perform automated checks and manual reviews daily. Major regulatory updates, permit changes, and ordinance bans are updated within 48 hours of municipal publication.

    Can I get notified when regulations change in my city?

    Yes! Click the "Subscribe to Alerts" button in the header, search for your location, and enter your email address to receive immediate updates when rules change.

    How do I suggest a city that isn't currently tracked?

    Click "Request Location" in the header to suggest a city, town, or county. Our research group will investigate the local laws and catalog it within 72 hours.

    Is this considered formal legal counsel?

    No. LawfulStay provides data for general informational purposes only. Local zoning codes can shift rapidly. We recommend checking with local planning boards or legal counsel before signing leases or purchasing investment property.
